Objective

The purpose of Team Griffin India is to innovate and excel in the field of aviation, particularly through the development of Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). With a focus on aerodynamics, avionics, manufacturing, and computer-aided engineering/design, the club aims to contribute meaningful applications of UAVs for societal benefit.

Achievements

Team Griffin India participated in a national drone development competition. Three teams were put in. One team won overall AIR 3 and the second team won overall AIR 6 and AIR 2 in Design Report. Recently we also conducted a drone building workshop in which students from VIT were taught how to build a drone from scratch.
